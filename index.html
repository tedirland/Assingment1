Ted Irland's Wickedly Awesome Portfolio

My Name is Ted Irland and I am an aspiring Software Developer. My career journey up to this point has been evolving to say the least.
I studied History and Spanish Language at Michigan State, hoping to go into the education field. During my time at MSU, I learned that I wasn't 100% passionate about becoming an educator. As most people who have no idea what they want to do often do, I took an entry level job at a large sales organization. Although sales was never something I was truly passionate about, I was good at it and learned much from some great leaders. I became a leader myself and went on to lead a very successful, if not fulfilling, 9 year career in the field.
In my sales career, I recieved the most satisfaction solving problems. In my mind, there is no better way to slove today's most pressing problems than with technology.
I work for Hired, which is a website that helps software developers find work. I asked my clients how they would recommend acquiring the skills I need to move into the software development field and the Trilogy bootcamp through Northwestern University came highly recommended!
